\section{Shooting Training}
\textbf{Cost:} 100 CP\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Passive, Repeatable, Source(100 Gold)}\\
When making a shooting weapon attack, you can add 1d4 to the attack roll.
