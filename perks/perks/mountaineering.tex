\section{Mountaineering}
\textbf{Cost:} 50 CP\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Skill, Passive, Source, Repeatable}\\
You can add your level to checks concerning climbing, navigation in mountainous areas, avalanches, and weather changes in the mountains.\\
\\
Level Progression:\\
II: 250 CP, add 1d4\\
III: 500 CP, add 2d4\\
IV: 1000 CP, add 3d4\\
