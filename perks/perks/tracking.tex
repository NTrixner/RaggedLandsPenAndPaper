\section{Tracking}
\textbf{Cost:} 50 CP\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Skill, Passive, Source(50 Gold), Repeatable}\\
You can add 1d4 to checks concerning tracks. This includes finding and following tracks and discerning the number and type of creatures from tracks.\\
\\
Level Progression:\\
II: 250 CP, add 2d4\\
III: 1000 CP, add 3d4\\
IV: 5000 CP, add 4d4\\
