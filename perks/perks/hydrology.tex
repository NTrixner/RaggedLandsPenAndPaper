\section{Hydrology}
\textbf{Cost:} 50 CP\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Skill, Passive, Source(50 Gold), Repeatable}\\
You can add 1d4 to checks concerning water. This includes fishing, swimming against currents, diving and holding one's breath, knowledge about maritime life and how currents move, etc.\\
\\
Level Progression:\\
II: 250 CP, add 2d4\\
III: 500 CP, add 3d4\\
IV: 1000 CP, add 4d4\\
