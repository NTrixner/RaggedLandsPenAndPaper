\section{Storytelling}
\textbf{Cost:} 20 CP\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Skill, Passive, Source, Repeatable}\\
You can add your level to checks concerning legend lore and reciting stories, fables, legends and songs.\\ 
\\
Level Progression:\\
II: 100 CP, add 1d4\\
III: 250 CP, add 2d4\\
IV: 500 CP, add 3d4\\
