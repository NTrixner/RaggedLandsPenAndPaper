\section{Anatomy}
\textbf{Cost:} 100 Gold\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Skill, Passive, Source, Repeatable}\\
You can add your level to checks concerning humanoid anatomy. This includes discovering the cause of death, discovering poison or disease, applying bandages, etc.\\
\\
Level Progression:\\
II: 400 Gold, add 1d4\\
III: 900 Gold, add 2d4\\
IV: 1500 Gold, add 3d4\\
