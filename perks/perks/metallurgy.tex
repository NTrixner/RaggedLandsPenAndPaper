\section{Metallurgy}
\textbf{Cost:} 100 CP\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Skill, Passive, Source(50 Gold), Repeatable}\\
You can add your level to checks concerning minerals like stone and metal. This includes identifying stone and metals, prospecting and mining ores etc.\\
\\
Level Progression:\\
II: 400 CP, add 1d4\\
III: 900 CP, add 2d4\\
IV: 1500 CP, add 3d4\\
