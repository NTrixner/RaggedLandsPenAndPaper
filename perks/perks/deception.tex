\section*{Deception}
\textbf{Cost:} 50 Gold\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Skill, Passive, Source, Repeatable}\\
You can add your level to checks concerning deception. This includes lying and discerning lies.\\
\\
Level Progression:\\
II: 250 Gold, add 1d4\\
III: 500 Gold, add 2d4\\
IV: 1000 Gold, add 3d4\\
