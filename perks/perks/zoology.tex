\section{Zoology}
\textbf{Cost:} 100 CP\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Skill, Passive, Source(50 Gold), Repeatable}\\
You can add 1d4 to checks concerning animals. This includes hunting for food, taming and handling animals, raising, riding and commanding animals etc.\\
\\
Level Progression:\\
II: 400 CP, add 2d4\\
III: 900 CP, add 3d4\\
IV: 1500 CP, add 4d4\\
