\section{Slashing Training}
\textbf{Cost:} 150 CP\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Passive, Repeatable, Source(100 Gold)}\\
When making a slashing weapon attack, you can add 1d4 to the attack roll.
