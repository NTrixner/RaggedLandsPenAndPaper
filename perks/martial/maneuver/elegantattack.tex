\section{Elegant Attack}\label{maneuver:elegantAttack}
\textbf{Cost:} 100 CP\\
\textbf{Requirements:} 3 Agility\\
\textbf{Maneuver, Active, Repeatable, Source(100 Gold)}\\
When making a weapon attack based on Agility, you can pay up to 3 Stamina and increase the weapon damage by that number. This damage increase is doubled for unarmed strikes.\\
