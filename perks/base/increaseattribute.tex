\section{Increase Attribute}\label{sec:increaseattribute}
\textbf{Cost:} 100 CP\\
\textbf{Requirements:} -\\
\textbf{Passive, Repeatable}\\
You increase one of your Attribute value by one, to a maximum of 12.\\
If you increase Strength, Intellect or Vitality, your Stamina, Mana or Health also increase by 1, plus 1 for each rank of ~\nameref{perk:increasestamina},~\nameref{perk:increasemana} or ~\nameref{perk:increasehealth} you have.
Each time you gain a higher rank for this perk, you increase an Attribute value by one point, to a maximum of 12.\\
