Size: L\\
Draw/Sheath: 7AP\\
Cost: 450 Gold

4 AP, ST to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, \passus{2} Reach\\
2d12 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes ST Piercing Damage

\textbf{Slash}\\
8 AP, ST to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, \passus{2} Reach\\
3d8 + \sfrac{1}{2}\texttimes ST Cutting Damage

\textbf{Block}\\
ST to block\\
2d6 + \sfrac{1}{2}\texttimes ST Cutting, Blunt or Piercing Damage
