\subsection{Sling}\label{weapon:sling}
Weapon, One-Handed, Simple, Ranged\\
Ammunition: Stones\\
Size: S\\
Cost: 10 Gold\\

\textbf{Draw/Sheath}\\
1AP draw, 2AP sheath

\textbf{Throw}\\
2 AP, DE or AG to hit, \passus{30} Reach:\\
2d6 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes AG Blunt Damage

\textbf{Reload}\\
2 AP\\
The Sling has to be reloaded after every shot\\
Reloading the Sling is a Two-Handed activity
