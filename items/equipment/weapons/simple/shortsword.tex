\subsubsection{Short Sword}\label{weapon:shortSword}
Weapon, Sword, One-Handed, Simple, Melee\\
Size: M\\
Cost: 75 Gold

\textbf{Draw/Sheath}\\
2AP draw, 3AP sheath

\textbf{Thrust}\\
2 AP, ST or AG to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, \passus{1} Reach\\
1d8 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes ST or AG Piercing Damage

\textbf{Slash}\\
3 AP, ST or AG to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, \passus{1} Reach\\
1d6 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes ST or AG Cutting Damage

\textbf{Block}\\
ST to block\\
1d4 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes ST or AG Cutting Damage
