\subsubsection{Great Club}\label{weapon:greatClub}
Weapon, Blunt, Two-Handed, Simple, Melee\\
Size: L\\
Draw/Sheath: None\\
Cost: 50 Gold

\textbf{Strike} \\
6 AP, ST to hit, \passus{1} Reach\\
2d10 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes ST Blunt Damage\\

\textbf{Block}
ST to block\\
1d4 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes ST Blunt, Cutting, or Piercing Damage
