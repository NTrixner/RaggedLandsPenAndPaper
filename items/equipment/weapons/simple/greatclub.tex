4AP/6AP

6 AP, ST to hit, \passus{1} Reach\\
2d10 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes ST Blunt Damage\\

ST to block\\
1d4 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes ST Cutting, Blunt or Piercing Damage
