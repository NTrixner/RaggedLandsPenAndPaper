\subsubsection{Club}\label{weapon:club}
Weapon, Blunt, One-Handed, Simple, Melee\\
Size: S\\
Cost: 20 Gold

\textbf{Draw/Sheath}\\
2AP draw, 4AP sheath

\textbf{Strike}\\
3AP, ST to hit, \passus{1} Reach\\
1d8 + \sfrac{1}{4}\texttimes ST Blunt Damage\\
When reducing a creature to 0 Health with this Attack, you can choose to knock them out instead of killing them.

\textbf{Block}\\
ST to block\\
1d4 + \sfrac{1}{4}\texttimes ST Cutting, Blunt or Piercing Damage
